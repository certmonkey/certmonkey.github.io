A lil' about me...

so back in mid 2019, I decided it would be a good idea to create a YouTube channel. I wanted to entertain people and make some cool content that people would want to watch and stuff. I had watched so many YouTubers and I had just randomly thought “yo, why don’t I become one of those dudes?” so that’s what I did.

once I created my first channel, my videos weren’t doing so well. but then a couple popped off and then they kinda blew up my channel. I made it to 300 or somethin’ subs, but then my channel started to die and then I just wasn’t dedicated to making videos anymore.

a couple years later in 2022, I started working on a new channel named ‘Cheezy Otta’. this time, I was more dedicated to uploading videos and told myself that I was gonna stick with this channel no matter what. and on may 15th, 2022, I uploaded my first video to that new channel.

after some time, the channel started to blow up. so instead of getting demotivated whenever I lost any subscribers, I kept trying my best to upload the best quality content that I could and do it as quickly as I could.

and if you’re visiting this website you probably know this channel today as ‘certified monkey’. so I’d like to sincerely thank all of the people who watched my videos and supported my channel to help build it to become what it is today.
