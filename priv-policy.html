Privacy Policy

At this website (https://certmonkey.github.io), there is certain information that I collect to help improve my services and make all of my users' experiences better. To learn about the information I collect and what I do with it, please read the policy below.

Information I Collect
I collect information from site visitors by using a 3rd party website analytics service known as 'Google Analytics'. With Google Analytics, I may track location data and device data about the device you use to enter this website.

This site also uses cookies to collect information.

Why I Collect Information
I collect information from site visitors to help improve all of my users' experiences. I use information about your device so I can find the most commonly used devices on this website and optimize it better for those devices.

I collect location data to know what countries/areas most site visitors come from so that I can add more localization settings for this site in the future.

How Long is Information Kept
Your information will be stored until January 1st, 2024. Every new year, old information is deleted from the database and new information will start to be collected until the end of that year.

What I Do With The Information
I keep the information in the Google Analytics database to study and help improve my users' experiences.

Your information will never be sold to anybody else.

However, your information may be shared with 3rd party services and business affiliates that include: Google Analytics and github.com/io.

Personal Information Protection
All of your information that is stored in this database is only available for me to view. Your personal information is protected with many layers of protection. Each set of data is assigned their own passwords and 2SV (via. google).

Do Not Use my Information
If you would like to opt-out of your personal information being collected/stored or if you would like to retrieve any of your personal information from the database, please contact cert.monkey.yt@gmail.com for assistance.

You are available to request to opt-out or retrieve your information at any time.

COPPA
The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act states that certain requirements must be made for collecting data from children under the age of 13.

On this website, any information collected by children under the age of 13 will be deleted immediately from the analytics database.

If you know that a child under the age of 13 has visited this site and data has been collected, please contact me immediately.

Site Ownership transferred
If site ownership is transferred, all information will be deleted from the analytics database; your device information and location information.

After ownership is transferred, the new owner(s) may start to collect information. If so, the new owners will create a new privacy policy that you should read.

Updates to This Policy
This policy may be updated from time to time. It is highly recommended that you frequently check this page for new updates to the policy.

Policy last updated on 24th July, 2023.

Contact
